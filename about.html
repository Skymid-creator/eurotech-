About Sorin.

A lifetime dedicated to precision.

Eurotech Camera Service was opened in May 1994 right here in Austin, Texas. But the journey began much earlier. I, Sorin Marcu, have extensive factory-level training and experience working on photographic and optical instruments.

Throughout my career, I have repaired and fully understood the mechanics of German, Swiss, Swedish, Russian, and Japanese cameras. Having worked alongside professors from the renowned German company, Carl Zeiss Optic, I developed a deep appreciation for uncompromising mechanical perfection.

"We learned to construct intricate camera components from scratch—a skill I carry into every restoration today."

Before launching Eurotech Camera Service, I worked in Austin for the largest photography store in the city in 1983, Studtman Photo. Running the service department, I specialized in rescuing everything from medium format Hasselblads to simple 35mm point-and-shoots.

Today, I continue to serve the analog community, keeping the golden age of photography alive, well-oiled, and perfectly calibrated.
